Kobe Bryant wins Oscar for animated "Dear Basketball"

The former Los Angeles Lakers star won an Oscar in the animated short category for "Dear Basketball,a poem he penned for The Players Tribune in 2015. He'll add it to an already jammed trophy collection that includes five NBA championships with the Lakers, two Olympic gold medals, NBA Finals most valuable player awards, a league MVP award and four All-Star game MVP awards.

Fergie issued an apology following her much-maligned National Anthem, telling TMZ in a statement, "I 've always been honored and proud to perform the national anthem and last night I wanted to try something special for the NBA.
